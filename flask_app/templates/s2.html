Happiness & Location

Geo Analysis with Twitter Data

This scenario involves geo-related analysis, as we investigated people's happiness score across different locations in Australia. Australian Statistical Geography Standard (ASGS) was used for statistical areas reference. Among the statistical standards, we used Statistical Areas Level 4 (i.e. Greater Capital City Statistical Areas), and Non ABS Structures (i.e. Suburbs and Localities).

In this scenario, our study mainly focused on answering the following questions with the happiness scoring algorithm:

  • Scenario 2.1: What's the average happiness score of Australia as a baseline?
  • Scenario 2.2: What's the happiness score of each states?
  • Scenario 2.3: What's the happiness score of greater-capital-city-statistical-areas & the rest area?
  • Scenario 2.4: What's the happiness score of suburbs and locations?

    Scenario 2.3

    Unveiling GCC's Happiness Across Australia

    What's the happiness score of Greater Capital City Statistical Areas and the rest of the areas?

    Key Findings:

    • Northern West part of Australia is happier than the Southern East part.
    • Apart from Greater Darwin, for each state, the Greater Capital Cities are happier than the Rest Areas.
    • Places above the Australia average happiness include: Greater Sydney, Rest of NSW, Greater Adelaide, Greater Hobart, Rest of Northern Territory, Australian Capital Territory.
