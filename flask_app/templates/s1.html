Happiness & Time

Non-geo Analysis with Twitter Data

This scenario involves non-geo-related analysis, as we investigated people's happiness score across different time period.
In this scenario, our study mainly focused on answering the following questions:

  • Scenario 1.1: What's the happiness trend from 2022.2 to 2022.8?
  • Scenario 1.2: What's the happiness trend by hour of a day?
  • Scenario 1.3: What's the happiness trend by day of week?
  • Scenario 1.4: What's the most frequent words related to happiness?
